# Repository content policy

## Allowed

- Original project source and documentation.
- Changes permitted by upstream licenses.
- Schemas, validators, stable error codes, and sanitized reports.
- Independently authored fixtures with provenance and license documentation.
- Nonrecoverable metadata such as hashes, counts, and format identifiers.

## Prohibited

Do not commit or upload:

- World at War executables, DLLs, FastFiles, maps, scripts, models, textures, sounds, animations, localization, or extracted game data;
- third-party custom maps, installers, archives, converted bundles, copied scripts, or extracted geometry;
- native framework binaries;
- leaked or decompiled proprietary source;
- private map-package research designated local-only;
- passwords, tokens, API keys, signing keys, or keystores.

## Fixtures

Every committed fixture must include provenance, license or permission, a review date, and a statement that it contains no stock or third-party protected content.

“Freely downloadable,” “abandoned,” “small,” or “used only for testing” does not establish redistribution permission.

## Generated outputs

Compatibility reports containing hashes, counts, classifications, failure codes, and sanitized performance results may be publishable after review. Converted third-party maps, reconstructed scripts or geometry, and transformed proprietary assets are not presumed distributable.
